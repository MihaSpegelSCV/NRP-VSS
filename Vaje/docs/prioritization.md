**Prioritization – MoSCoW**

**Izbrana metoda**

Uporabljena je metoda **MoSCoW**, ker omogoča jasno razdelitev funkcionalnosti glede na pomembnost za MVP.

---

## Razlaga

* MUST → nujno za delovanje produkta
* SHOULD → pomembno, vendar ne kritično
* COULD → dodatna vrednost
* WON’T → prihodnje funkcionalnosti

---

## Razvrstitev

### 🔴 MUST

* Scan link
* Phishing warning
* Explanation
* Steps after detection
* Paste message
* Safety score
* Verified domain
* History

---

### 🟡 SHOULD

* Daily tips
* Reporting phishing
* Recent scans
* Quick access
* Open safe links
* Learning from history

---

### 🟢 COULD

* Score history
* Notifications
* Gamification (XP)
* Personalized tips

---

### ⚪ WON’T

* Bank integrations
* Enterprise features
